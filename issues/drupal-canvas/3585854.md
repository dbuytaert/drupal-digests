## Technical details

The issue tracked 20 `npm audit` vulnerabilities in the Canvas monorepo's JavaScript dependency tree. The fix bumps numerous packages in `package-lock.json` and tightens version constraints in `package.json` files for `packages/cli` and `packages/workbench`. Notable updates include: `axios` from ^1.9.0 / ^1.7.8 to ^1.15.1 (with a corresponding `proxy-from-env` major bump from 1.1.0 to 2.1.0, a transitive dependency), `follow-redirects` 1.15.11 to 1.16.0, `dompurify` 3.3.2 to 3.4.0 (also dropping its Node 20 engine constraint), `lodash` and `lodash-es` 4.17.23 to 4.18.1, `brace-expansion` v1 1.1.12 to 1.1.14 across multiple dependents, `astro` 5.18.0 to 5.18.1, `vite` 6.4.1 to 6.4.2 and 7.3.1 to 7.3.2, `hono` 4.12.8 to 4.12.14, and several others. A `yaml: ^2.8.3` override was added to the root `package.json` to deduplicate the four separate `yaml` 1.x shims that had been installed under `oas-linter`, `oas-resolver`, `oas-validator`, and `swagger2openapi`, as well as the `yaml-language-server` pinned 2.7.1 entry; all are now satisfied by the hoisted `yaml` 2.8.3.
