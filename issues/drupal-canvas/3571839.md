The diff shows a comprehensive update to `package-lock.json` that upgrades `glob` from `^11.1.0` to `^13.0.1` in the root `package.json` overrides section, and updates `rimraf` from `^6.1.0` to `^6.1.2` in the `packages/create` package. The `glob` 11.1.0 version was showing deprecation warnings about security vulnerabilities. Version 13.0.1 removes dependencies on `@isaacs/cliui`, `jackspeak`, `foreground-child`, `package-json-from-dist`, and other transitional packages that were part of glob's CLI infrastructure.

The update also brought in minor version bumps for many other packages in the lockfile including `@astrojs/compiler`, `@babel/generator`, `@types/node`, `@types/react`, `caniuse-lite`, `get-tsconfig`, `magicast`, `semver`, and `unrun` among others.
