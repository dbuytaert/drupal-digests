## Summary

Drupal CMS now ships a `drupal_cms_privacy_basic` recipe that gives every new site a working cookie consent setup out of the box. Applying it installs the Klaro Cookie and Consent Manager with sensible defaults, creates published Privacy Policy and Imprint pages at `/privacy-policy` and `/imprint`, and places footer menu links to both pages. Anonymous and authenticated users are granted the `use klaro` permission automatically. When at least one Klaro app is enabled, a "My privacy settings" link appears in the footer so visitors can review or withdraw consent. The consent modal links to the site's own privacy policy page. The original feature request proposed a broader data protection suite covering PII reports, encryption, and data export sanitisation; the advanced recipe (MR !191) was ultimately closed without merging. What landed is the foundational privacy layer that all Drupal CMS site templates now build on.

## Impact

Affects module maintainers.

- **New API:** New `drupal_cms_privacy_basic` recipe provides consent management, default Privacy Policy and Imprint pages, and Klaro cookie consent integration for Drupal CMS sites.

## Technical details

The `drupal_cms_privacy_basic` recipe (`recipes/drupal_cms_privacy_basic/`) installs `klaro`, `eca_base`, `eca_config`, `eca_core`, `eca_misc`, `eca_ui`, `menu_link_attributes`, and `modeler`. Its `recipe.yml` applies `simpleConfigUpdate` actions on `klaro.settings` to enable `auto_decorate_final_html` and `library.group_by_purpose`, grants `use klaro` to both `user.role.anonymous` and `user.role.authenticated`, and ships default content for the Privacy Policy and Imprint nodes plus their footer `menu_link_content` entries. The `klaro.texts` action that points `consentModal.privacyPolicy.url` to `internal:/privacy-policy` was intentionally moved out of this recipe and into `drupal_cms_site_template_base`'s `recipe.yml`, so site templates can control the privacy policy URL while the base recipe stays independent of a specific page alias. The recipe was included as a required dependency of `drupal_cms_site_template_base` (the foundation for all Drupal CMS site templates including Blank) after the foundational recipe consolidation in MR !930, which also removed the standalone `drupal_cms_authentication` and `drupal_cms_admin_ui` recipes. A `ComponentValidationTest` verifies idempotent application, correct `klaro` permission grants, and that the "My privacy settings" footer link appears once a Klaro app is enabled.
